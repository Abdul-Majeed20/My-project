How does this work?

Thanks to our direct partnerships with leading freezones in the region,
we’ve made the process super simple!

01

Quick consultation call

Schedule a 1:1 call with our team to talk through your ideal corporate and personal setup. You can skip this step if you’d like to!

02

Submit 4 documents

Once you’ve paid the registration fees, and submitted
these documents we convert them to ideal format for the
freezone and petition for a license.

03

Get your business license!

In 3 weeks after submitting your documents, we’ll email
you your business license. You can now issue a visa for
yourself or setup a bank account.
